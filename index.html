Golden Ale

Golden Ales, also called Blonde Ales, are relatively young compared to other styles in the beer world.

IPA

India pale ale (IPA) is a hoppy beer style within the broader category of pale ale.

Lager

Lager is beer which has been brewed and conditioned at low temperature. Lagers can be pale, amber, or dark.

Porter

Porter is a style of beer that was developed in London, England in the early 18th century

Stout

Stout, dark, heavy-bodied beer popular in Great Britain and Ireland.

Weissbier

Weissbier is the classical wheat beer of Bavaria and one of Germany's greatest and most distinctive beer styles.
